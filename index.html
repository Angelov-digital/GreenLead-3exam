Projects

Hydropower investment in Greenland

INVESTOR GUIDANCE, TECHNICAL-ECONOMIC ANALYSIS, NAVIGATING THE POLITICAL ENVIRONMENT

With many years of experience with hydropower in Greenland, we offer help in finding hydropower potentials from 5 MW - 1000 MW for use in sustainable production. We help with concept development, project partner matching, contact with authorities, and economic-technical analysis of projects.

Heat batteries 0.5 - 300 MWh

ELECTRIFICATION OF PROCESS HEAT AND HEATING PLANTS

For industry and utility companies, compact modular heating batteries are offered for conversion of heat from gas, oil or biomass to electrical supply. Electricity is collected every day when it is cheapest, and is used the rest of the day. The closed heating medium can be both water, oil and air.

Energy advisory and optimization

COMPLETE SOLUTIONS WITH SUN, WIND, INSULATION AND STORAGE

GreenLead helps your company with the green energy transition. We calculate and put together complete solutions within PV solar, solar heating, wind turbines, insulation of buildings, control and battery solutions for both electricity and heating. We can advise or take care of the entire conversion project for you.

Utility consulting

BUSINESS CONSULTING FOR OF UTILITY COMPANIES

With several years of experience as directors of Greenland's national utility company, we now advise utility companies on operations, organization, systems, project setup, green conversion, as well as management and financial management. Our professional knowledge spans electricity, water, heat, production and distribution as well as political governance.

Nationail energy systems

NATIONAL SOCIO-ECONOMIC ANALYZES

Analyzes at national level of supply systems, in electricity, heat, water, production, distribution, backup, peak load, standby, connection, introduction of new technology/products. We carry out socio-economic impact analyses, calculations of price systems and scenario calculations.

Project development for the green transition

ENERGY SYSTEMS FOR UTILITY AND INDUSTRY

Sustainable energy is a key word in the world's development, towards a zero-emission society. It is a tremendously exciting challenge and a pretty big mouthful for many companies, municipalities, countries, and right down to the individual consumer. We have worked on calculations, project planning and scaling of energy systems in the Arctic in Greenland and in Denmark, for energy-intensive industries, urban supply and local optimization from oil to electricity. We are passionate about the green energy transition, with our heads firmly in place, so that economy and sustainability go hand in hand.
